How to Buy LadyGo (LGO)

Note: LadyGo (LGO) is currently in its presale phase and is not available on Uniswap, PancakeSwap, or any other decentralized exchanges (DEXs). Please be cautious and avoid purchasing any fake LGO tokens that may be circulating on DEXs, as they are scams.

Note:If you dont have ETH on base network then you need to bridge ETH or transafer ETH to Base network

How to Buy

Step 1: Prepare Your Wallet

Before participating in the Ladygo presale, ensure you have a wallet supported by Wallet Connect. We recommend using MetaMask or Trust Wallet, but any decentralized wallet compatible with Wallet Connect will work.

Step 2: Get ETH

Make sure you have Base (ETH) in your wallet, as it is required for gas fees on the Basenetwork, regardless of whether you are purchasing with USDC.

Step 3: Connect to the DApp

1. Open your preferred web browser and visit https://presale.ladygo.taxi/.
2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button.
3. Ensure you are on the BaseChain (ERC20).

Step 4: Choose Payment Option

On the Ladygo dashboard, select your preferred payment option by clicking the appropriate button (ETH or USDC).

Step 5: Enter Purchase Amount

1. Enter the amount of ETH or USDC you want to invest in the Ladygo presale.
2. The dashboard will display how many LGO tokens you will receive for the selected amount.
3. Confirm your selection and click the "Buy" button to proceed.

Step 6: Verify and Confirm Transaction

1. Your wallet (e.g., MetaMask) will display the transaction details.
2. Verify that the transaction details are correct, including the amount and recipient address.
3. Confirm the transaction within your wallet.
4. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the Basenetwork, which may take time depending on network congestion.

IMPORTANT TO NOTE: When purchasing with USDC, users must complete two transactions:
1. The first transaction is to approve the USDC contract.
2. The second transaction is for the actual purchase amount.
Ensure both steps are completed to finalize your transaction.

Step 7: Purchase Successful

Once the transaction is confirmed, your LGO tokens will be sent to your wallet in real-time.
